# Global Superstore — Sales & Profit Insights Dashboard

## Overview
Interactive **Tableau dashboard** analyzing **$12.6M in global retail sales** across regions, customer segments, product categories, and shipping logistics. Built to surface actionable insights for strategic business decisions.

## Tool
- **Tableau Desktop / Tableau Public**

## Dashboard Components
1. **Regional Sales Breakdown by Customer Segment** — Bar chart showing sales by region and customer type
2. **Top 10 Most Profitable Sub-Categories** — Horizontal bar chart ranking profit by product
3. **Monthly Sales Trend by Segment** — Line chart with trend lines across 4 years
4. **Days to Ship vs. Profit** — Analysis of shipping time and profitability correlation
5. **Profit by Ship Mode** — Comparison of shipping methods by profit and product category

## Key Findings
- Central region generated $2.8M+ in Consumer segment revenue
- Copiers and Phones were the most profitable sub-categories ($258K and $216K respectively)
- Standard Class shipping yielded the highest profit margins, especially for Technology products
- 4–5 day shipping window correlated with peak profitability
