# Global Superstore — Sales & Profit Insights Dashboard

## Overview
Interactive **Tableau dashboard** analyzing **$12.6M in global retail sales** across regions, customer segments, product categories, and shipping logistics. Built to surface actionable insights for strategic business decisions.

## Tool
- **Tableau Desktop / Tableau Public**
- https://public.tableau.com/views/GlobalSuperstoreSalesandProfitInsightsForStrategicGrowth/GlobalSuperstoreDashboard?:language=en-US&:sid=&:redirect=auth&:display_count=n&:origin=viz_share_link

## Dashboard Components
1. **Regional Sales Breakdown by Customer Segment** — Bar chart showing sales performance across 13 regions, segmented by Consumer, Corporate, and Home Office
2. **Top 10 Most Profitable Sub-Categories** — Horizontal bar chart ranking profit by product type
3. **Monthly Sales Trend by Segment** — Line chart with trend lines across 4 years of monthly data
4. **Days to Ship vs. Profit** — Analysis of shipping time impact on profitability
5. **Profit by Ship Mode** — Comparison of First Class, Same Day, Second Class, and Standard Class by category

## Key Findings
- **Central region** generated $2.8M+ in Consumer segment revenue — strongest regional performer
- **Copiers and Phones** were the most profitable sub-categories ($258K and $216K respectively)
- **Standard Class shipping** yielded the highest profit margins, especially for Technology products ($405K)
- **4–5 day shipping window** correlated with peak profitability, suggesting optimized logistics cost structures
- **Consumer segment** showed the strongest growth trend over time
